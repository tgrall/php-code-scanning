Title: JS Obfuscation


Concept / Topic To Teach:

Obfuscation is a means of "obscuring" the real meaning and intent of your javascript code. Some sites use it as an obstacle to people who want to copy/borrow their code. Other sites use it as a means to hide the actual intention of the code. This is not a good approach for verifying passwords and user can easily get the password.

General Goal(s):

Your goal is to get the correct password.
